Match the Hatch.
Generate the right number to 'match the hatch' and catch the fish.
You will be given a random number (the fish) at the start of the game.
There are four flies below. By clicking on a fly you will "make a cast" and add a specific amount of points to your score.
You win by matching your total score to the random number (and using the right fly combination!), you lose if your score goes above the random number.
The value of each fly is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each fly.
